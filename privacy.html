Privacy Policy for CaptionEdge

Effective Date: 15 March 2025

CaptionEdge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CaptionEdge handles your data.

1. No Automatic Data Collection

CaptionEdge does not automatically collect, store, or transmit any personal data, media files, or usage analytics. All app features run entirely on your device, and no photos, videos, or captions are uploaded to the cloud.

2. Local-Only Processing

All photo and video captioning is performed on-device. Your content:

  • Stays on your device and is never transmitted externally.
  • Is used solely to generate captions locally.
  • Remains private and fully under your control.

3. Feedback and Optional Data Collection

If you choose to contact us through the app to provide feedback or report an issue, we may collect:

  • Your email address (if provided),
  • App version,
  • Device model and OS version,
  • Basic usage context (e.g., recent app actions or errors), to help us respond to your inquiry and improve the app.

This data is only sent with your explicit action and is used exclusively for support and troubleshooting purposes. It is not shared with third parties.

4. No Third-Party Tracking or Advertising

CaptionEdge does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or any tracking technologies.

5. Permissions

CaptionEdge may request permission to access your photos and videos in order to generate captions. This permission:

  • Is used only for local processing.
  • Can be revoked at any time via your device's privacy settings.

6. Children's Privacy

CaptionEdge is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top. Any material changes will be communicated through the app or App Store listing.
