Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our photo captioning technology

Do my pictures leave my phone when I use your application?

No. All your pictures stay on your phone all the time. All processing is done on the phone. No pictures or data leave your phone.

Does your application use any wireless data?

The only time our application uses wireless data is to download the AI model. Once that is done, all processing is done on the phone and it does not need to access any external data. If you send us feedback, those would be sent via email which does use wireless data.

Why are older phones not supported?

Unfortunately, Picription uses a Large Vision AI model, which requires modern hardware for proper operation. Older phones do not have fast enough CPU or sufficient memory to support that, but you can use iPhone that is less than 3 years old.
